About


The Hogwarts Project is a new, high functioning startup working towards the end goal of building a full scale Hogwarts Castle. Our team consists of people from diverse backgrounds, with expertise ranging from licensing to marketing. We're driven by being able to create something that will give kids, teens, and adults a once in a life time experience, particularly those living in underpriveleged communities.


It's a priority to have the final castle be exactly as it is in the movies, to recreate as much of the magical experience as possible. Once completed, Hogwarts will hold 600 students (150 in each house, then divided into the seven years). Students will be sorted into houses by random, and age determines the year group. THP will also staff 100 people, with the main staff being food prep, teachers, housekeepers, and Head of Houses.


                                              The waitlist for Hogwarts starts Summer 2021

Stages of Project

Stage 1  (Nov 2020 - Mar 2021)

Set up construction firms, licensing rights, full schedule, and benefactor


Stage 2 (Jun 2021 - May 2022)

Extensive planning and details, blueprints, land purchases


Stage 3 (Jun 2022 - July 2024)

Construction starts, castle begins to go up, Quidditch stadium construction


August 2024 - Hogwarts opens
